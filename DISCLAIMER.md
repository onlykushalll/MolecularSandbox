# ⚠️ Scientific & Liability Disclaimer

This repository, **The Molecular Sandbox**, is a virtual chemical simulator designed strictly for educational, illustrative, and learning purposes. 

---

## 🚫 Not a Guide for Physical Experiments
Under no circumstances should any reaction equations, temperature profiles, precipitation outputs, kinetics data, or titration curves generated by this software be used as a literal guide, procedure, or instructional manual for conducting physical laboratory experiments.

Real-world chemical mixtures involve dangerous variables not fully modeled in a virtual sandbox, including but not limited to:
*   Exothermic runaways, sudden pressure releases, or toxic gas evolution.
*   Contaminants, physical degradation of containment vessels, and spontaneous decomposition of materials.
*   Flammability hazards and varying specific conditions (e.g. ambient humidity, localized heating).

## 🔬 Theoretical Modeling Limitations
The algorithms used in the chemistry engine, stoichiometry calculations, Arrhenius equations, and pH calculations are based on idealized models and standard thermodynamic constants:
*   Enthalpy values ($\Delta H$) and pH calculations assume standard temperature and pressure (STP) and complete dissolution, which may differ significantly in actual laboratory settings.
*   Visual effects (such as precipitate particles, gas drifts, or thermal shock breaking probabilities) are visual approximations intended to aid learning and are not precise physical representations.
